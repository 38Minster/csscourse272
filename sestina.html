The Sestina

Despite, or perhaps because of the difficulty of writing meaningfully in this strict form, it is popular with contemporary poets

the sestina consists of thirty-nine lines split into six stanzas - each stanza has six lines and there is an envoi of three lines at the end. Usually a sestina does not rhyme - in fact it is unusual for it to do so
